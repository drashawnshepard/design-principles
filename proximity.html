Proximity

Placing items in proximity to one another goes back to clumping. The idea is to avoid a busy, cluttered layout by physically grouping items together that belong together. Proximity must be used wisely in order to be effective (60).

Proximity is a tough principle to use effectively. If you have too much content in one space, it is confusing. But if you put things together that work together well, your content becomes much better.

Proximity is used to put content together to create deeper meaning. If cars should be in the same category, they are placed in the same category. If they are dissimilar, it will not make sense.
