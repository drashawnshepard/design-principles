Continuity

Continuity takes the eyes where they need to go. Eyes will follow the direction of a line. Applying continuity adds a flow to your layout.

The eyes are very important for content creator. If the eyes go to the wrong place, it can ruin the message of your content. Continuity make things smoother and more unified.

Many ad companies use continuity to bring readers to their product. This is a powerful tool that good companies master. If they can't bring eyes to their product, then they have nothing.
