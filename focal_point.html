Focal Point

The focal point is the center of attention in the design or layout. Without a focal point, the viewer doesn't know where to look first (50). The focal point can be anything really, as long as it remains the most eye-catching piece of the visual information (51).

The focal point may be the most important part of any effective design. It grabs the audiences attention and brings them in. An effective focal point allows your content to be more consumable because the audience is already invested.

Focal points are extremely important for creating good content in today's world. Whether it be the thumbnail for a youtube video or the headline of a news article, Focal points set the audience attention on a singular subject. Yet, that's just the first step towards creating effective content.
