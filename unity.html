Unity

Unity means that all the parts of the design work together, and everything looks like it belongs together. A layout is visually unified if its different parts have visual links or relationships to one another. Unity is oneness (55).

If you're content is not unified, real problems arise. The audience won't be able to concert their attention. You most likely will lose their attention.

All good designs are unified. If you use all of the elements but they do not work together it will not work. If you miss an element it will not work.
