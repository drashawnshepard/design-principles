Contrast

Contrast is simply the arrangement of opposite elements. You can employ contrast in many different ways in your design. It just takes putting two opposites together.

Contrast is a great way to make content more interesting. Putting white text on a black background or vice versa is an easy way to make your text pop. Using different types of patterns throughout content keeps the audience engaged.

Contrast makes your content more interesting and therefore allows you to have more effective content. If your audience is looking at something interesting, they are more likely to continue. Contrast accomplishes that goal.
