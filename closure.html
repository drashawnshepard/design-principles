Closure

We mentally fill in the gaps in order to complete a perceived shape. The idea of designing with only part but having your viewer perceive the whole opens up interesting compositional opportunities, including the interplay of positive and negative space.

People will see a picture based on what they see. Giving the reader an answer without actually giving them the answer creates more engaging content.

Many companies incorporate this into their logo, whether it be NBC or Google. An engaged audience is a good one, and good content desigers know that.
