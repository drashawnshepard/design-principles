Similarity

Our minds group thing with similar properties, such as color and shape. "Like goes with like." We can use similarity to create order and organization through unity.

Certain things work together well. Effective content uses similar things to convey a message. Similarity is important in creating unified design.

Places all around us use similar things to organize items. Without similarity, there is no order. Content without order is not worth entertaining.
