Rhythm

Movement created by patterns is called rhythm. In graphic design, rhythmic movement has to do with repeating items strategically. You can create repetition with fonts, colors, or even how you position certain elements (59).

Patterns create a way to keep the audience engaged. If you use items well, this will be accomplished. Many different elements can be used to create rhythm.

Many well built and well styled companies use rhythm because it keeps the reader involved during movement. Its not just a straight line to where they are going. It becomes a fun journey.
