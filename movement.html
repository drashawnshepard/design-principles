Movement

Good design controls the eye's flow through the composition. The flow of lines can move the eye across the page or screen. Lines, then, can create movement, and different kinds of linear movement tend to communicate different kinds of symbolic messages (57-58).

Aligning text and images in specific ways allows you to manipulate how the reader looks at your content. This gives you power as a content creator to make certain things happen. Mastery of movement in content design is crucial in conveying the message you are looking to send.

Movement is used in many ads and videos today, to keep the audience interested. Frames will move from one place to another really quickly allowing content creators to tell a story. If they have the audience's attention, the eyes will do the rest.
